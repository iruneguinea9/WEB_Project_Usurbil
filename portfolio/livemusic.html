Taylor Swift

Taylor Alison Swift is a multi-Grammy award-winning American singer/songwriter who, in 2010 at the age of 20, became the youngest artist in history to win the Grammy Award for Album of the Year. In 2011 Swift was named Billboard's Woman of the Year. She also has been named the American Music Awards Artist of the Year, as well as the Entertainer of the Year for both the Country Music Association and the Academy of Country Music, among many other accolades. As of this writing, she is also the top-selling digital artist in music history.

Haize Berriak Banda

Haize Berriak Banda is coming back to Usurbil. As the concert they played last year was a complete success, Haize Berriak Banda of Sakana offers their new show called "Katius", as the conductor David Sanchez has stated, this show is aimed for children but people from all ages can enjoy it. Katius is a musical story, narrated by Belen Otxotorena.

Haize Berriak Banda is turning 25 next year and to celebrate, they are offering special discounts to their public! They are also making giveaways in their instagram account. Click HERE to check it out
