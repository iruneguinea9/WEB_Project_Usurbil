Other Events

  • Belen Otxotorena

    Sunday, 6th December 16:15

    "The secret of the blue wind" is a show written and preformed by Belen Otxotorena, with the help of FBMN's (Navarre's Music Band Federation) symphonic band, Belen narrates the stoty of blue wind, a character who helps all the birds fly. When blue wind gets captured by the evil Cronos, the birds must go find blue wind without their ability to fly. With the music of very famous composers such as Jacob de Haan, Vicent Egea or Arturo Márquez, this show is an experience that you can't miss!

  • Peter and the Wolf

    Saturday, 5th February 18:30

    Peter and the Wolf is a musical composition written by Sergei Prokofiev in 1936. The narrator tells a children's story, while the orchestra illustrates it by using different instruments to play a "theme" that represents each character in the story. It is Prokofiev's most frequently performed work and one of the most frequently performed works in the entire classical repertoire.

  • Carnival workshop

    Sunday, 6th March 16:30

    Time to get ready for the Carnival! In this workshop guided by Ramon Paniagua, kids will have the oportunity to make their own costumes and accesories for the Rural Carnival. From Momotxorros to Witches, kids will have the resources and guidance needed to make their costume stand out from the crowd.
